Welcome to CRC Chambers of Law LLP

CRC Chambers of Law LLP is India's leading litigation law firm with in-depth expertise to handle litigation pertaining to Criminal, Civil, Writ Petitions, NCLT, NCLAT, RERA, DRT, Consumer Commission, Land Acquisition matters, Arbitration, Narcotics, CBI, Economic Offenses Wing (EOW), Family Courts and other related matters.

Founded by Mr. Surender Singh Pannu and Mr. Naresh Kumar Kalia in 2017, we operate five offices across India - three in the National Capital Region (NCR) and two in Tricity (Chandigarh, Panchkula). Over the years, we have come to dominate the litigation landscape of the country and have serviced a wide variety of clients.

We began our services with a predominant focus on cases in the Supreme Court, High Court of Delhi, Punjab & Haryana High Court, Himachal Pradesh High Court, and Bombay High Court. We have since expanded our legal vision, ensuring presence in all Subordinate Courts, Tribunals, and Commissions of the NCR, Tricity and Shimla regions.

At CRC Chambers of Law LLP, we believe in offering measured and thought-through legal advice for our clients in an expeditious and cost-effective manner. The client is always that of the firm and never of an individual, thereby ensuring the most optimum, client-friendly utilization of our wide range of talent. With two founding partners and about 55 lawyers, we deliver comprehensive legal solutions tailored to each client's unique needs.
