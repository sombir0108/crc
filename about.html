About CRC Chambers of Law LLP

Leading India's Litigation Landscape Since 2017

Our History

CRC Chambers of Law LLP was founded in 2017 by Mr. Surender Singh Pannu and Mr. Naresh Kumar Kalia with a vision to dominate the litigation landscape of India through excellence, expertise, and unwavering commitment to client success.

We began our services with a predominant focus on cases in the Supreme Court, High Court of Delhi, Punjab & Haryana High Court, Himachal Pradesh High Court, and Bombay High Court. Over the years, we have expanded our legal vision, ensuring that we have a presence in all Subordinate Courts, Tribunals, and Commissions of the National Capital Region (NCR), Tricity (Chandigarh, Panchkula) and Shimla.

Today, we operate five offices across India - three in the NCR and two in Tricity. We have expanded our presence across specialized Tribunals and subordinate courts like the National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT), National Company Law Appellate Tribunal (NCLAT), Consumer Commission, Competition Commission of India, National Green Tribunal, Debt Recovery Tribunal (DRT), and Real Estate Regulatory Authority (RERA).

With two founding partners and about 55 lawyers at present, we have come to dominate the litigation landscape of the country and have serviced a wide variety of clients. Our expertise spans Criminal litigation (including Narcotics, CBI, and Economic Offenses Wing cases), Civil litigation, Writ Petitions, Arbitration matters, Family Courts, Land Acquisition matters, and other related legal proceedings.
