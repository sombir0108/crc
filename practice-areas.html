Our Litigation Practice Areas

Specialized expertise in complex litigation matters across all courts and tribunals

CRC Chambers of Law LLP is India's leading litigation law firm with in-depth expertise in handling complex litigation matters across Criminal, Civil, Corporate, Family, Arbitration, and specialized tribunal proceedings. Established in 2017, we have come to dominate the litigation landscape of the country.

Our practice spans the Supreme Court, High Courts (Delhi, Punjab & Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Bombay), and all Subordinate Courts, Tribunals, and Commissions in NCR, Tricity, and Shimla regions. With two founding partners and about 55 lawyers, we deliver strategic litigation solutions tailored to each client's unique needs.
