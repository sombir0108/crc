Our Leadership Team

2 Founding Partners leading a team of 55+ Lawyers

CRC Chambers of Law LLP is led by two founding partners, Mr. Surender Singh Pannu and Mr. Naresh Kumar Kalia, who bring decades of combined litigation expertise. Together with a team of about 55 lawyers, we deliver exceptional legal services across Supreme Court, High Courts, and Tribunals throughout India.

Our team has dominated the litigation landscape through meticulous preparation, strategic thinking, and unwavering commitment to our clients' success. Each member of our firm brings specialized expertise in their respective practice areas.

Founding Partners

Surender Singh Pannu

Founding Partner

Specialization: Supreme Court & High Court Litigation

Mr. Surender Singh Pannu is the co-founder of CRC Chambers of Law LLP, established in 2017. With extensive experience in handling complex litigation matters, he leads the firm's practice before the Supreme Court and multiple High Courts including Delhi, Punjab & Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, and Bombay High Courts.

His expertise spans across criminal litigation, civil matters, writ petitions, and appellate advocacy. Under his leadership, the firm has grown to dominate the litigation landscape of the country, handling high-stakes cases across various legal domains.

Naresh Kumar Kalia

Founding Partner

Specialization: Tribunals & Appellate Litigation

Mr. Naresh Kumar Kalia is the co-founder of CRC Chambers of Law LLP. He brings extensive expertise in tribunal proceedings, particularly before NCLT, NCLAT, RERA, DRT, Consumer Commission, Competition Commission of India, and National Green Tribunal.

Together with Mr. Pannu, he has expanded the firm's presence across all Subordinate Courts, Tribunals, and Commissions in NCR, Tricity, and Shimla regions, building a team of about 55 lawyers dedicated to litigation excellence.
